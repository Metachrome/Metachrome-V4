Privacy Policy – Metachrome

Effective date: October 26, 2024  •  Last updated: October 26, 2024

Summary (TL;DR): This Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, how long we keep it, and your rights—especially under the EU/UK GDPR. If you disagree with this Policy, please do not use the Platform.

1) Who We Are & Scope

Metachrome (“Metachrome”, “we”, “us”, “our”) operates metachrome.io and related applications (the “Platform”). This Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data of individuals who visit or use the Platform and the services we provide (the “Services”).

For GDPR purposes, Metachrome is the data controller for processing described in this Policy unless stated otherwise.

2) Contact & Data Protection

Contact (including DPO queries): privacy@metachrome.io or support@metachrome.io. If required by law, we may appoint a representative or a Data Protection Officer (DPO); you can reach them via the same email.

3) Definitions

Personal data means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. Processing means any operation performed on personal data, e.g., collection, storage, use, disclosure, deletion.

  • Directly from you: forms, KYC flows, customer support, surveys, promotions.
  • Automatically: cookies, SDKs, pixels, server logs, and similar technologies.
  • From third parties: identity verification providers, sanctions/PEP screening, payment processors, analytics and ad partners, fraud prevention vendors, affiliates.
  • Social media & integrations: when you interact with widgets, OAuth, or referral links.

7) Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies, local storage, pixels and SDKs for essential functions, analytics, personalization, and (where applicable) advertising. You can manage preferences via your browser settings and our cookie controls (if available). Blocking some cookies may impact functionality. See our Cookie Notice (if provided) for details.

8) Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers/processors: hosting, KYC/AML, payments, analytics, security, communications, storage, support.
  • Affiliates/group companies: for operations consistent with this Policy.
  • Business transfers: in mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales (your data may transfer with the business).
  • Legal & compliance: to comply with law, lawful requests, sanctions obligations, or to protect rights, property, users, or the public.

9) International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, which may have different data protection laws. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), UK IDTA/Addendum, and additional measures when necessary. You can request a copy of relevant safeguards via privacy@metachrome.io.

10) Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest (where appropriate, e.g., AES‑256 for sensitive fields).
  • Access controls, audit logging, and least‑privilege permissions.
  • Network protections (firewalls, IDS/IPS), vulnerability scanning and patching.
  • Incident response program and regular risk assessments.

No system is 100% secure. We implement safeguards appropriate to the risk and will notify authorities/users where legally required in case of a breach.

11) Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, including meeting legal, accounting, and reporting requirements (e.g., KYC/AML retention periods typically 5–10 years depending on jurisdiction). When no longer needed, we will delete or irreversibly anonymize it.

12) Your Rights (GDPR & Similar)

Subject to conditions and local law, you have the right to:

  • Access your data and obtain a copy;
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Erase data (where applicable);
  • Restrict processing in certain cases;
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing;
  • Data portability (receive data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format);
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
  • Complain to your supervisory authority.

Requests: contact privacy@metachrome.io. We may need to verify your identity. We aim to respond within one month (extendable where permitted).

Automated decisions: Where automated decision-making or profiling has legal or similarly significant effects, you may request human review, express your point of view, and contest the decision.

13) Marketing Choices

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking “unsubscribe” or emailing us. You may still receive essential transactional/operational messages. For cookies used for advertising, adjust your browser/device settings and our cookie controls (if available).

14) Children

The Platform is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it where required by law.

15) Third‑Party Sites & Services

The Platform may link to third‑party websites, services, or plug‑ins. Their privacy practices are governed by their policies, not this Policy. Review those policies carefully.

16) Do Not Track

Some browsers offer “Do Not Track” signals. Because there is no common industry standard, our Platform may not respond to DNT signals. You can control cookies via your browser.

17) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change and, where required by law, you will be notified. Your continued use of the Platform after changes signifies acceptance.
